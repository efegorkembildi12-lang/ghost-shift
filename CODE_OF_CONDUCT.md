# Code of Conduct

Ghostshift is committed to a respectful, collaborative, and low-ego community.

## Our Standards

- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Give feedback about ideas and code, not people.
- Avoid harassment, intimidation, or exclusionary behavior.
- Make room for learners, first-time contributors, and non-native English speakers.

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate these standards and may take further action when needed to protect contributors.

## Reporting

Security-sensitive or conduct-sensitive concerns can be reported privately to the maintainers once the project publishes its contact channel. Until then, open a private conversation through the repository host rather than escalating in public threads.
