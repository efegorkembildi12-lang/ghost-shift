# Launch Messaging

## Positioning

### One-Line

`Ghostshift is the open-source audit trail for AI-made work.`

### Short Version

`Git shows what changed. Ghostshift shows why an AI agent changed it, how to replay it, and how to inspect it later.`

### Three Proof Points

- `blame --line` shows semantic and decision context for a changed line
- `replay` and `compare` make AI work inspectable and repeatable
- the self-host preview UI reads open export data with no hosted dependency

## Messaging Rules

- do say `open-source audit trail for AI work`
- do lead with commands, workflow, and concrete inspection value
- do not say `AI observability platform`
- do not lead with abstract category language
